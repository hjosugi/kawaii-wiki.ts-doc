# Organization and Search

## Hierarchies

The `/` in a path acts as a folder. Example: `product/design/buttons`. In the page tree on the left, you can open and close folders and use stars, personal order, and shared order.

## Labels and Spaces

Labels are suited for tagging across multiple classification axes, while spaces are intended for separating permissions and large areas. Consider using labels before creating overly deep hierarchies.

## Search

You can use normal phrases, `"exact phrase"`, `-excluded word`, and filters for title, status, labels, spaces, and creators. Trigram search is suitable for Japanese text.

## Bidirectional Links and Graphs

Backlinks are displayed at the bottom of pages. The graph helps visualize connections between pages and find pages that have not yet been created. You can toggle it on and off with "Show/Hide Graph" in the reading view, and your choice is saved on your device. It is fine to keep it off while reading the main content.
